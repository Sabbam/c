Biography

A Post-Graduate in Computer Science& Technology from Andhra University College of Engineering(A), Visakhapatnam, Mr.Farhatullah is presently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of CSE, Dr.L.Bullayya College of Engineering, Visakhapatnam since 2012. He has a Total Teaching experience of more than 15 years, teaching Computer Science Engineering courses to Undergraduate students of Engineering in affiliated colleges of JNTU and Andhra University. He qualified in the APSET and UGC-NET examinations conducted in 2012.

Education

Master of Technology in Computer Science Technology, from Andhra University College of Engineering (A), Visakhapatnam.

Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science & Engineering, from NCET, Vijayawada, affiliated to J.N.T.University, Kukatpally.

Experience
