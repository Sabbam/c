Skill Development Center (SDC)

Skill Development Center is not a new concept and its roots can be traced back to ancient times. However, Skill Development in its present form, owes its origin to US and other developed countries. Skill Development encompasses information, guidance and counseling services to assist in making educational training and occupational choice. Skill Development and counseling programmes in SSCE aim to provide assistance and advice to students to make them more powerful and better informed so that they can become architects in building their own future. It helps the students realize their strengths and weaknesses by instilling self awareness, decision making skills, planning skills, personality development etc. A separate cell, with a well-equipped air-conditioned conference training room and separate well-furnished rooms for mock interviews and counseling, has been set up,. encouraging students to sharpen their skills and make them 'Industry Ready.'


Training, Placements and Career Guidance Cell is a core area in an Engineering College which complements the efforts of the members of faculty by organizing value-added programs and providing placement opportunities to students. the cell continuously coordinates with various industries and organizations for the placements of the students. It functions with coperate sectors and helps in develoin the industries-academic inter-relationship. The training and placement Officer makes regular visits to various companies, on an all Indian basis, to project the capability of the students skills to fulfill the requirements of the corporate world. Full-fledged training and placement facilitates leading MNC's for campus selections


Objectives

To help students share knowledge about themselves by identifying skills, and interests.

To provide information about further course prerequisites, financial aid, academic planning, entrance examinations etc.

To promote career guidance & counseling through lectures by senior corporate executives and visiting professors.

To organize seminars on interview skills, personality development, communication skills, leadership skills, resume writing, analytical skills, quantitative ability, verbal and reasoning skills essential to all competitive exams.

GRE/TOFEL

Special training is provided to students who are aspiring for higher education abroad. It focuses on Verbal, Quantitative and Reasoning skills along with Analytical Writing Assessment. A good number of students from different branches utilized the services and progressing in different universities abroad.
