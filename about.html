About College

About College


Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering encourages and promotes education and keeps its main focus on Student empowerment and their personality development with which they can face the challenges of modern world. The broad objective of the institution is to provide the right opportunities to the students for learning the higher skills in new technologies and building their extraordinary careers in industry and research organizations.

Acclaimed by the student community as an outstanding engineering college in the smart city Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering (for women), was established by The Society for Collegiate Education in 2010 with the objective to produce women technologists and scientists who would engage in research, design and development to help building the nation. Our Campus is ideally located in the heart of the city on 10 acre land.

About College

The campus life is a perfect learning ambience to aspiring students with facilities such as well-staked modern e-library, large seminar halls, fully furnished laboratories, up to date language laboratories, spacious class rooms, multi-sport play Ground, health centre and cafeteria. The Wi-Fi & Internet includes continuous and uninterrupted internet connectivity to students and faculty members. The Internal Quality Assurance Cell constantly monitors the academic progress of students and the teaching efficiency of the teachers.


Proper care is taken to develop student’s overall personality, bringing out the students hidden virtues through activities such as NCC, NSS, Sports, Nature Club, literary and cultural competitions. The College enjoys outstanding success in inter-university sports competitions producing many top class sports personalities.


Safety & Security of students is the primary concern of the college. It is a highly Secured and Sophisticated Campus which provides UV treated safe drinking water for entire campus and well experienced security personnel positioned at all the gates. The entire college campus is kept under the surveillance of CCTV Camera for making the college a ragging free campus.


The students who are seeking to improve their technological knowledge, transform themselves into promising personalities or enrich their lives through scientific education, Dr. L. Bullayya College of Engineering is the right choice for them. The college fraternity feel honored to partner with such aspiring students in their educational journey. The students, staff and the members of the governing body, encourage you to learn more about us by visiting our campus.
