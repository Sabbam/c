Tap Cell

Objective

From our own assessment as well as from the observations of several academicians, technicians, technocrats and industry people, it has been found that there is a gap between the skills acquired by the students of engineering and the skills required by the industries. We, therefore, wish to bridge the gap by imparting the reqired skills to our dear studens of engineering so as to enable them to aspire for better opportunities in employement and education To arrange GATE/ GRE/ TOEFL/ ILETS/ GMAT/ CAT coaching classes.


Training, Placements and Career Guidance Cell is a core area in an Engineering College which complements the efforts of the members of faculty by organizing value-added programs and providing placement opportunities to students. the cell continuously coordinates with various industries and organizations for the placements of the students. It functions with coperate sectors and helps in develoin the industries-academic inter-relationship. The training and placement Officer makes regular visits to various companies, on an all Indian basis, to project the capability of the students skills to fulfill the requirements of the corporate world. Full-fledged training and placement facilitates leading MNC's for campus selections


functions

To arrange Seminars/ Workshops on career oppurtunities.

To arrange GATE/ GRE/ TOEFL/ ILETS/ GMAT/ CAT coaching classes.

To conduct mock tests for Competitive examinations.

To arrange interactive sessions between alumni and students.

To procure study material and make it available in the library/ Intranet.

Training

It was divided into two parts Internal and External


Internal

Internal Training is being given with the help of seasoned and experienced professionals in reasoning, quntitative aptitude and soft skills with personality development to train the students to reach the appropriated skills for the indusrties or for higher studies


External

External Training to the students will be provided by the external agencies like Orion Edutech, Talent Sprint, Smart, India Times, Gate Forum etc. to meet the requirements of our objectives.
